Plate Heat Exchanger Upgrades

To optimize performance to meet current process requirements with minimum energy consumption, Alfa Laval offers three plate heat exchanger upgrade services through our highly qualified service engineers. Ensures plate and gasket materials meet current process media and temperature requirements thereby prolonging the service interval between plate and gasket replacement. This helps reduce downtime and extend plate service lifetime.

Centrifuge Services

Alfa Laval centrifuges operate on a high RPM resulting in very high “G force” resulting in efficient purification or clarification of the liquid to be treated. It then becomes imperative to ensure that the centrifuge is being run in top condition by ensuring use of Alfa Laval genuine spare parts and following a Preventive Maintenance Schedule as recommended by Alfa Laval to also ensure the safety of the equipment and that of the personnel operating the equipment. We, at Value Concepts, ensure timely supply of spares so that your process is run in the most optimized way. We can do minor to moderate centrifuge repairs at customer site.
